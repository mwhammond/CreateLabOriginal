Imperial Create Lab

An Imperial wide community interested in exploring ideas, meeting other innovative, creative people and developing the skills and connections to create amazing things. Typically over pizza.


How it works

We bring together events ranging from easy going socials and CEO talks to ‘Invent the future’ events in which industry experts help you to come up with game-changing ideas. All of this is wrapped together with courses to help you fill in any skills gaps (business, prototyping, apps etc.), alongside the mentorship and connections to help get your ideas off the ground.


Inspiration

Talks, socials, Invent the Future events, co-founder speed networking, hackathons and startup weekend. Invites are sent via email and social media so sign up below.

Skill up

Get the skills you need to turn your ideas into reality from business to app design 3D prototyping. Watch the social media for invites.

Start-up

Show there’s real interest in your idea and you can get access to 1:1 guidance, expert mentors, industry connections, investors and office / prototyping space. Apply via any member of the team at an event.

Startup

If you find an idea that you think you could form a business around then we can provide tailored help to get it off the ground as a viable alternative to a graduate job. Due to cost and the fact that we don't take equity we are limited to 30 ventures per year so you will need to prove that there is customer interest. If you don't already know how test interest before building anything keep an eye out for the business skills workshops. Apply by talking to any of the team at events.


Fact: Last year over 200 teams entered the pitch event, over 30 of those are still on their way to changing the future and have raised over £1m in funding to date.
