We are an Imperial wide community interested in exploring ideas, meeting other innovative, creative people and developing the skills and connections to create amazing things. Typically over pizza.

How it works

We bring together events ranging from easy going socials and CEO talks to ‘Invent the future’ events in which industry experts help you to come up with game-changing ideas. All of this is wrapped together with courses to help you fill in any skills gaps (business, prototyping, apps etc.), alongside the mentorship and connections to help get your ideas off the ground.


Insiration

Talks, socials, Invent the Future idea gen, co-founder speed networking, hackathons, startup weekend. Invites are sent via email and social media so sign up below.

Learn

Get the skills you need to turn your ideas into reality from business to app design 3D prototyping. Watch the social media for invites.

Start-up

Show there’s real interest in your idea and you can get access to 1:1 guidance, expert mentors, industry connections, investors and office / prototyping space. Apply via any member of the team at an event.

Next events

You can join in as much or as little as you like, from coming along to socials to learning how to start a businesses. Events are released via email and social media and typically fill up within hours so if these sound good you may wish to consider signing up below.
